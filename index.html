About Edvance

Edvance was founded by Lina Mirghani, an educator passionate about educational technology, artificial intelligence, and the future of learning. Lina graduated with a Master’s in Education and Technology from University College London (UCL) Institute of Education and has worked across the education sector in a variety of roles. Her experiences have shaped Edvance’s mission: to make technology and AI accessible in every classroom, while keeping educators at the heart of innovation.

We believe that technology should always serve pedagogy. Our team combines years of expertise in education, technology, and artificial intelligence to deliver practical and sustainable solutions for both schools and teachers. We support educators as they adapt to new tools and approaches in a rapidly evolving digital world.
